### CAD Designing

CAD (Computer-Aided Design) is an essential process for hardware product-based startups, enabling them to bring their ideas to life in a virtual environment. Our team of skilled designers utilizes advanced CAD software to create detailed 2D and 3D models of your product. Through CAD designing, startups can visualize, iterate, and refine their product concepts before moving on to the manufacturing stage. With precise measurements, material selection, and component placement, CAD designing streamlines the development process, saving time and costs while ensuring accuracy and manufacturability.

### CAD Designing

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="2D Drafting">


2D drafting involves creating technical drawings using CAD software. It focuses on precise dimensions, annotations, and detailing. It is commonly used for architectural plans, circuit diagrams, and mechanical drawings.
</Tab>

<Tab name="3D Modeling">



3D modeling refers to the creation of three-dimensional digital models using CAD software. It allows for visualizing the product in a virtual environment. Complex surfaces, geometries, and assemblies can be accurately represented. It is widely used for designing products, architectural renderings, and visual simulations.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Assembly Design">

Assembly design involves creating virtual assemblies by integrating individual parts. It helps visualize the interaction between components, check for interferences, and assess assembly feasibility. It is crucial for designing complex mechanisms, machinery, and products with multiple components.

</Tab>

</Tabs>
